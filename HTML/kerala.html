Famous Food of Kerala

Appam & Stew

Appam with stew is a traditional breakfast of Kerala with soft rice pancakes served with vegetable or meat stew.

Kerala Sadya

A traditional feast served on banana leaves with rice, sambar, curries, and desserts. A must-try cultural experience.

Puttu & Kadala Curry

Puttu is a steamed rice cake served with spicy chickpea curry. A healthy and delicious Kerala specialty.
