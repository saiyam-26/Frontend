About Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise with crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious overwater bungalows. Ideal for honeymooners, adventure seekers, and nature lovers, the Maldives offers snorkeling, diving, island hopping, and serene sunsets that are truly unforgettable.

Famous Food of Maldives

Maldivian Fish Curry

A traditional Maldivian curry made with fresh tuna, coconut milk, and local spices. Served with rice or flatbread.

Mas Huni

A traditional Maldivian breakfast made with tuna, coconut, onion, and chili, typically eaten with flatbread called roshi.

Boshi Mashuni

A local dish made with banana flower, coconut, and spices. Savory and rich in flavor.
