About Goa

Goa, the party capital of India, is famous for its **stunning beaches**, **vibrant nightlife**, **Portuguese architecture**, and **seafood cuisine**. Ideal for beach lovers, adventure seekers, and culture enthusiasts, Goa offers everything from serene sunsets to exciting water sports.

Famous Food of Goa

Xacuti Curry

A traditional Goan curry made with spices, coconut, and meat or seafood, often served with rice or **sanna (Goan steamed rice cakes)**.

Goan Prawn Curry

A flavorful prawn curry cooked in **coconut milk** and spices, a must-try dish for seafood lovers, giving a distinctly tropical taste.

Feni

A local alcoholic drink made from **cashew** or **coconut**, unique to Goa, classified as a "country liquor."
